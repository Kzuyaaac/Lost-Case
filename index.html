Chess Empire - A Chess Shooter Game

Platform: Android

Release: September 2024

Developer Team:

  • Zahir Raihan Hakim (Project Manager, Art, Designer)
  • Arvand Cahil (AI Developer)
  • Ilyasa Daffa (Game Designer)
  • Fabio Mana' Ahil (Main Programmer)
  • M. Zafran Al-Majid (VFX Artist)

Game Description:

Chess Empire is a strategy game inspired by Shotgun King. It combines traditional chess with exciting shooter mechanics. Players control the black king, armed with a shotgun, and face enemies—white chess pieces—that move according to chess rules. The game challenges players' strategic thinking and tactical skills.

Key Features:

  • Intuitive Android controls for smooth mobile gameplay.
  • Collect skill cards to transform into other chess pieces with special abilities.
  • Multiple shotgun types and king skins for customization.
  • Progress through increasingly challenging levels with a well-crafted storyline.
  • Follow King Kael's journey to defend the kingdom of Yonarion from dark forces.

Storyline Summary:

You play as King Kael, the last hope for Yonarion, defending it from the corrupted kingdom of Auroria. Using his strategic mind and powerful shotgun, Kael must fend off enemies turned into zombie-like creatures by dark powers.

Gameplay Mechanics:

  • Move the king and shoot enemies with the shotgun.
  • Use transformation skill cards to become different chess pieces.
  • Fully designed for mobile—no keyboard inputs required.
