Lost Case - A Horror Detective Game

Platform: PC

Engine: Unreal Engine

Developer:

  • Zahir Raihan Hakim (Programmer, World Building)
  • Arvand Cahil (Programmer, Game Optimisation)
  • Ilyasa Daffa (Game Designer, Programmer)
  • Fabio Mana' Ahil (Programmer, AI Developer)
  • M. Zafran Al-Majid (Story)

Description:

Lost Case is a chilling horror detective game where you play as a skilled detective investigating an old, unsolved missing-persons case in a mysterious forest. Your mission is to uncover the truth behind these disappearances while facing eerie environments, challenging puzzles, and terrifying anomalies lurking in the dark.

Key Features:

  • Stunning Graphics: Experience eerie realism powered by Unreal Engine.
  • Puzzle Mechanism: Solve intricate puzzles to progress through the story.
  • Horror Elements: Prepare for spine-chilling jump scares and unexpected encounters with anomalies and monsters.
  • Survival Mechanics: Manage your health, watch your surroundings, and use limited resources wisely.

Development Story:

This game was developed by a group of five friends, with no dedicated designer. We heavily relied on Unreal Marketplace assets, but also created some assets ourselves. This made the project a challenging yet exciting experience!

Assets Used:

  • Lookout Tower
  • Post-Soviet Village
  • ASC Teuthisan

Gameplay Instructions:

Explore the haunted forest, investigate key areas, and solve puzzles to uncover the truth. Be cautious, as you never know what might be lurking in the shadows. Use your detective skills and survival instincts to complete the case.
